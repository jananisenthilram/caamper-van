Packing Checklist

Pack Smart For The Road

Keep your road trip smooth with the essentials every Wander Vans traveler should bring before heading out.

Travel Essentials

  • Driving license and ID proof
  • Booking confirmation and route details
  • Wallet, cards, and emergency cash
  • Phone charger and power bank

Clothing & Comfort

  • Layered clothes for changing weather
  • Comfortable shoes and sandals
  • Jackets, caps, and sunglasses
  • Personal toiletries and towels

Food & Daily Use

  • Reusable water bottles
  • Quick snacks and dry foods
  • Basic cooking supplies
  • Tissues, wipes, and trash bags

Safety & Outdoors

  • First-aid kit and medicines
  • Flashlight and insect repellent
  • Blanket or light sleeping gear
  • Weather-ready rain cover
