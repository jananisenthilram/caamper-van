Be Prepared

Essential Packing Guide

Everything you need for a comfortable, stress-free camper van adventure.

Clothing & Gear

  • Layered clothing for all weather
  • Waterproof jacket & boots
  • Comfortable hiking shoes
  • Sun hat & sunglasses

Kitchen Essentials

  • Reusable water bottles
  • Dry & canned food supplies
  • Portable spice kit
  • Cutting board & knife set

Safety & Health

  • First aid kit
  • Insect repellent & sunscreen
  • Prescription medicines
  • Emergency contact list

Tech & Navigation

  • Power bank & chargers
  • Offline maps downloaded
  • Dash cam & action camera
  • Bluetooth speaker
