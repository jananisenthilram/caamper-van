Day by Day Plan

Road Trip Itinerary

A sample 5-day camper van journey from Chennai to Goa - plan yours just like this.

Day 1

Chennai Salem

340 km  |  ~5 hrs drive

Start your journey from Chennai. Drive through the scenic NH44 highway, stop at Krishnagiri for lunch and reach Salem by evening. Set up camp near the reservoir.

Lunch Stop Overnight Camp
Day 2

Salem Coimbatore

160 km  |  ~3 hrs drive

Morning drive through the Mettur Dam area. Reach Coimbatore by noon, explore the local markets and enjoy a relaxed evening at a campsite near the Nilgiris foothills.

Dam Visit Local Market
Day 3

Coimbatore Goa (via Gokarna)

580 km  |  ~9 hrs drive

Long drive day - head north along the coast. Stop at Gokarna beach for a refreshing break. Continue to South Goa and park your camper at a beachside campsite.

Beach Stop Beach Camp
Day 4

Explore Goa

Full Day  |  No Drive

Rest day in Goa! Visit Palolem and Agonda beaches, explore Old Goa churches, enjoy fresh seafood and local shacks. Evening bonfire at the campsite.

Seafood Bonfire Heritage
Day 5

Goa Return

Return Journey

Morning sunrise at the beach, pack up the camper and head back. Drop off the van at the nearest pickup hub or arrange a one-way return with our team.

Sunrise Return
Day 1

Bangalore Mysore

150 km  |  ~3 hrs

Drive from Bangalore to Mysore via the expressway. Visit Mysore Palace and Chamundi Hills. Camp near Brindavan Gardens.

Palace Camp
Day 2

Mysore Ooty

120 km  |  ~3 hrs

Drive through the Bandipur forest reserve into Tamil Nadu. Ascend the Nilgiris to reach Ooty. Explore the botanical gardens and tea estates.

Forest Tea Estate
Day 3

Explore Ooty

Full Day

Visit Doddabetta Peak, Ooty Lake and the famous Nilgiri Mountain Railway. Enjoy the cool weather and local cuisine before heading back.

Toy Train Lake
Day 1

Mumbai Alibaug

100 km  |  ~2 hrs

Start from Mumbai and drive south to Alibaug. Visit Kolaba Fort and enjoy the quiet beaches. Camp by the sea for a peaceful night.

Fort Sea Camp
Day 2

Alibaug Ganpatipule

220 km  |  ~4 hrs

Drive along the Konkan coast through lush green landscapes. Reach Ganpatipule, visit the famous temple and relax on the pristine beach.

Temple Beach
Day 3

Ganpatipule Return

Return Journey

Morning beach walk, fresh coconut breakfast and pack up. Drive back to Mumbai via the scenic Konkan highway with memories to last a lifetime.

Scenic Drive Return
